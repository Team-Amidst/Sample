The MVP

Manny Pangilinan

Headed by Manny V. Pangilinan

Mr. Pangilinan's contribution to the society is big deal. He donated face masks, personal protective equipment (PPEs), and 14,000 litres of 70 per cent ethyl alcohol to frontliners and govern- ment-owned hospitals. Through his many connections, he was able to help and distribute grocery packs to the families of health workers and many more. Truly undeniable to appreciate their kindness and being helpful whilst in the pandemic.
